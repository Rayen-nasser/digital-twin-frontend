How to use AI Avatar Streaming:
  1. Configure your preferred avatar, voice, and language settings
  2. Enter an initial script for the avatar to speak when starting
  3. Click "Start Streaming" to begin a new session
  4. Or click "Generate & Stream from Chat" to create content from your chat history
  5. Type messages to interact with the AI avatar in real-time
  6. The avatar will speak the responses using the selected voice
